About

I am Hacettepe University Computer Education and Instructional Technologies Student, I currently hold the role of President at the Knights Boardgame University Community, where I foster a shared passion for strategy and innovation.

My interests extend beyond the academic sphere into the realms of Graphic Design and Art, which I pursue as enriching hobbies. I have also ventured into the world of video game development, harnessing the power of Unity to bring imaginative concepts to life. My experience as a Scrum Master in a game development project has honed my skills in team management and project coordination. Moreover, I enjoy creating 3D models with Blender, further expanding my digital artistry. While I am proud of the skills I have acquired, I am a firm believer in lifelong learning. I am a quick learner and an efficient multitasker, traits that have allowed me to master numerous programs with ease. As evident in the Skills section, my journey of self-improvement is ongoing. In the future, I aim to develop my own portfolio bootstrap to further demonstrate my growing capabilities.
